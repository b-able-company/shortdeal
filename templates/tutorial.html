How to Use ShortDeal

Welcome to ShortDeal! This guide will help you get started with our short-form content trading platform.

üöÄ Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

First, you need to sign up for a ShortDeal account. Choose your role:

  • Producer: If you want to sell your short-form content
  • Buyer: If you want to purchase short-form content

Sign Up Now

üé• For Producers

After signing up as a Producer, you'll need to complete onboarding:

  • Enter your company name
  • Add a description of your business
  • Set up your booth (your storefront on ShortDeal)

This information helps buyers understand who you are and what content you create.

Go to My Content in the navigation menu and click Upload New Content:

  • Add a compelling title and description
  • Upload your video file
  • Add a thumbnail image (optional)
  • Choose categories/tags
  • Set content status (Draft or Public)

You can upload multiple pieces of content and manage them all from your dashboard.

When buyers are interested in your content, they'll send offers:

  • View all offers in Received Offers
  • Review offer details (price, terms, buyer information)
  • Accept or reject offers
  • Negotiate terms if needed

Each offer includes detailed information to help you make informed decisions.

When you accept an offer, an LOI (Letter of Intent) is automatically generated:

  • Review the auto-generated LOI document
  • Sign the LOI to formalize the agreement
  • Access all LOIs from LOI Documents in the menu

The LOI protects both parties and ensures a transparent transaction.

üõí For Buyers

After signing up as a Buyer, complete your profile:

  • Enter your company name
  • Describe your business and content needs

This helps producers understand who they're working with.

Find the perfect content for your needs:

  • Browse all content from Browse Content
  • Use search and filters to find specific content
  • Click on content to view details
  • Visit producer booths to see their portfolio

You can browse content even without logging in, but you'll need an account to make offers.

When you find content you want to purchase:

  • Click Make Offer on the content detail page
  • Fill in your offer details:
    • Offered price
    • Intended use of the content
    • Additional terms or requirements
  • Submit your offer to the producer

Be clear and specific in your offer to increase chances of acceptance.

Monitor all your offers from My Offers:

  • View offer status (Pending, Accepted, Rejected)
  • When accepted, sign the LOI document
  • Access all agreements from LOI Documents

Keep track of all your negotiations in one place.
